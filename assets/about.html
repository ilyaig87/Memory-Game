About I-Games

A little corner of the web built by people who love games.

Welcome to I-Games! We're a team of gaming enthusiasts who are passionate about all kinds of games, from timeless board classics to clever little browser challenges.

Everything here runs right in your browser — no downloads, no sign-ups, no cost. We built each game by hand, and our Chess, Checkers and Backgammon games feature a genuine, thinking AI opponent so you always have a worthy challenge, even on your own.

We hope you enjoy exploring the collection. If you have questions, comments or ideas, we'd love to hear from you.
